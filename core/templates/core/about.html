About Faidi MMF

Faidi Money Market Fund (Faidi MMF) is Kenya’s premier investment platform. Our mission is to grow your money safely, consistently, and transparently. With daily returns of 0.05% (~15% monthly), we empower our investors to achieve financial freedom while maintaining full control and visibility over their funds.
