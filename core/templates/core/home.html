FAQs

Questions about FAIDII

What is FAIDII MMF?

FAIDII MMF is a digital investment platform that helps users deposit funds, invest, monitor growth, and view transaction records from one account.

How much do I need to start?

You can start with a minimum deposit of KES 100, then invest from KES 2,500 upward using your available wallet balance.

How does investment profit work?

Each investment earns 2.5% profit daily. Daily profits are credited to your wallet automatically and can be withdrawn from your available balance.

How do deposits work?

Deposits are initiated through M-Pesa STK push. After confirmation, the deposit is recorded in your FAIDII transaction history.

Can I withdraw my money?

Yes. Daily profits can be withdrawn from your available wallet balance. The principal remains locked for 7 days, then returns to your wallet automatically.
